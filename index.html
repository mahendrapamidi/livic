Acumen-Civil

Acumen is the annual technical fest of Vasavi College of Engineering, Hyderabad. With footfalls of over 2000 witnessed in 2012,13,14.It is well known for being one of the biggest and best technical fests in Hyderabad. With a plethora of events, ACUMEN CIVIL promises to be a platform for ideation, creativity and innovation. It allows the students to break free from the monotony of classroom learning. We strongly believe in what Roosevelt once opined on Civil Engineers: “Far better it is to dare mighty things, to win glorious triumphs, even though checkered by failure, than to rank with those poor spirits who neither enjoy much nor suffer much, because they live in the gray twilight that knows not victory nor defeat.” Civil engineers today will serve as master builders, environmental stewards, innovators and integrators, managers of risk and uncertainty, and leaders in shaping public policy. Acumen is one step we take towards nurturing this ambitious dream!!!! The 18th edition of the two day fest will be held on 7th March,2015. With exciting prizes and never seen before events, Acumen Civil promises to surpass all its previous editions!!!
